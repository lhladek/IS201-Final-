PRAGUE

I served my mission in the Czech Republic for 18 months. For part of my mission, I served in Prague, one of the most beautiful and oldest cities in the world. I am passionate about learning more about Prague and its rich culture.


Facts about Prague:

  1. Must-see Landmarks:
    • Charles Bridge
    • The Prague Castle
    • Old Town (Staromeska)
    • The Astronomical Clock
    • Letna


  2. Fun Facts:
    • In 2021, Prague was voted the #1 most beautiful city in the world.
    • The Prague Castle is the largest castle in the world.
    • Prague is the city where beer is consumed more per capita than any other city in the world.

Prague's Prices:

As shown in the chart below, Prague is a fairly cheap city compared to other big cities in the world. The currency in the Czech Republic is called a Korun, or crown in english. One US dollar is equal to about 21 crowns. Prague is an excellent place for travel due to its low cost of living and low prices.
